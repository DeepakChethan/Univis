2K11ME 808 SEMINAR 

Class: 3 Hrs / week Internal Assessment Marks: 50 The seminar course is intended to build both written and oral communication skill of a student. It is also intend to develop self confidence and remove stage fear in a student. Each student is required to make visual presentation using PPT on any technical topic relevant to mechanical engineering after carrying out necessary information retrieval to mechanical engineering after carrying out necessary information retrieval from journals, reports, internet, etc. Each student has to make one preliminary presentation and one final presentation after getting approval of topic of seminar from the faculty assigned. The internal assessment marks will be awarded by the assigned faculty member based on the performance of the student in the seminar and submission of seminar reports.
