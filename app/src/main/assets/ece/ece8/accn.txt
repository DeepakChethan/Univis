2KEC804 – ADVANCED COMPUTER COMMUNICATION NETWORKS

Basics of discrete and continues time Markov chains: Poisson Processes, Queuing theory M/M/I, M/GI/I queue, stationary distribution stability conditions, little’s Law. -15hrs 

Performance analysis scheduling algorithms, MAC protocols, Slotted ALOHA, CSMA. -10hrs

MPLS, QoS for real and data applications, DffServ and InterServ architectures. - 6hrs

Introduction and issues in Sensor Networks. - 5hrs
